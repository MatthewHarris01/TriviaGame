Space Exploration Trivia

Directions

To start the game, click the "Start Game" button.

Each question will be shown on the screen for about 20 seconds.

Possible answers to the question will be shown in a list under the question. Answer choices will be highlighted when you hover the mouse over them.

Click one of the answers to choose that answer as your answer to the question

After you click an answer, the game will tell you whether your choice was the correct answer, or an incorrect answer

If you don't choose an answer before the question timer expires, the game will move on to the next question

Whether you answer correctly, incorrectly, or your time expires, the game will display the correct answer to the question.

The answer will remain on-screen for about 10 seconds, and then the game will display the next question.

When you have gone through all of the questions, the game will show your total score.

You score will be shown for about 10 seconds, and then the game will start another round of play, and show you all the questions again.

Close or refresh the page to stop game play.

Have Fun!!
